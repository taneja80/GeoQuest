About GeoQuest

GeoQuest is an educational geography app that makes learning about the world fun and interactive — for kids and curious adults alike!

🌍 Countries

Browse detailed profiles of 195+ countries — flags, capitals, populations, languages, currencies, and more.

🗺️ Continents

Explore all 7 continents and the countries within each one. Understand how the world is divided geographically.

🔭 Discover

Dive into oceans, mountain ranges, rivers, deserts, forests, volcanoes, and the wonders of the world.

🎮 Quiz Game

Test your geography knowledge with 4 question types: capitals, flags, population, and area. Earn points, beat the leaderboard!
